Redesign of JoyofBaking

In this course, I worked on a website Information Architecture redesign which was an individual term project. JoyofBaking was selected to be redesigned. The process started with analyzing and evaluating the current design from IA perspectives. Based on the analysis, personas and requirements were developed. With requirements, I proposed solutions to solve problems. The deliverables of wireframe can be found at Axure share

Deliverables:

  • Strategy and Scope
  • Evaluation of website’s information architecture
  • Proposed Redesign
  • Interactive prototype
MusicNow

The MusicNow is a mobile application that connects performers to local business and fans. This application allows performers to showcase themselves via customized portfolios and to search for opportunities provided by local business. Fans use this system to follow performers and provide their feedback.

Methodology: Conceptual Modeling, Persona, Scenario, Prototyping, Questionnaire, Interview, Usability Evaluation
RoamWithMe

The RoamWithMe is a project that aims to develop a system to facilitate computer-supported collaborative work (CSCW). Target users are travelers who love to share their experiences. This system enables users to create their own network for information sharing with fellow travelers, to connect with local stores and share their locations with private groups.

Methodology: Conceptual Modeling, Personas, Scenario, Prototyping, Questionnaire, Interview, Usability Evaluation
SavvyShop

The SavvyShop project aims to develop an application that brings users better shopping experiences. Key features include price comparing, shopping list management, in-store route assistance and budget management. Contextual inquiry was a major method to observe users’ shopping processes and elicit user requirements.

Methodology: Contextual Inquiry, Affinity Diagram, Storyboard, Scenario, Prototyping, Questionnaire, Interview, Usability Evaluation
WeatherBug Redesign

The WeatherBug is an existing application for weather forecasting. This application provide full aspects of weather information from temperature to lifestyle forecast. Users would benefit from these features to their decision makings. Our project focused on the redesign of the current system in order to increase user satisfaction. We utilized diary study to understand users’ behaviors and cumbersome situations in order to elicit user requirements.

Methodology: User Diary, Affinity Diagram, Storyboard, Scenario, Prototyping, Interview, Usability Evaluation
BalanceIt

The BalanceIt is a project to design a game for Sifteo cubes. This game requires users to manipulate physical cubes to finish tasks. We focused on how to utilize a prototyping method during the design process with physical limitations. For early stages, we used sketches and low fidelity paper prototypes to explore possible ideas. High fidelity prototypes were used at later stages to examine user flows in details. To ameliorate our design, we conducted user testing with users to find out issues so that we could revise this game into smoother processes.

Methodology: Sketching, Prototyping, Interview, Usability Evaluation
STAR

Most people read articles from the Internet. Since the number of articles are increasing rapidly, retrieving an article might become more difficult without proper cues. People tend to classify article in a computer folder system. However, as the mobile device market grows, the mobile device usage grows. In the STAR project, we investigated different cues when people retrieve articles that they read. Based on our research, we aimed to develop an application to assist users to finish these retrieving tasks efficiently.

Methodology: Prototyping, Interview
OneTravel

Our team chose a travel reservation website, OneTravel, for this project. During preliminary reviews, issues were identified by expert inspections and were confirmed later by results from user testing. A competitive analysis was also conducted in the evaluation process to consolidate the strengths and weaknesses of The OneTravel website. We made suggestions based on our finding in order to improve the usability of the current website.

Methodology: Heuristic Evaluation, User Testing, Data Analysis, Interview
